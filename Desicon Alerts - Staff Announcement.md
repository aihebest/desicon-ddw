## Version 1 — Company-wide email (main announcement)

**Subject:** Action not required — New "Desicon Alerts" notifications now coming to your screen

Dear Colleagues,

As part of our drive to improve internal communication, ICT is rolling out **Desicon Alerts**, our new official platform for delivering important company information directly to your computer screen — including HSE and safety alerts, ICT notices, HR circulars, project updates, and company-wide announcements.

The goal is simple: make sure important messages reach you quickly and clearly, instead of being missed in a crowded inbox.

**What you need to do: nothing.** The application is being installed automatically on Desicon computers. There's nothing for you to download.

**What you will notice over the next few days:**

- The first time it starts, you'll be asked to **sign in once** using your normal Desicon Microsoft 365 account (your work email and password). This is expected and safe.
- You'll be asked to confirm your **office location and department** once, so you only receive alerts relevant to you.
- A small **information icon** will appear near the clock, at the bottom-right of your screen.
- From time to time, **alert pop-ups** will appear on your screen. Simply read them and click **"Got it"** (or **"Acknowledge"** for important safety notices) to close them.

Please note that some critical alerts — such as HSE or emergency notices — will ask you to acknowledge that you've seen them. This helps us confirm that everyone received vital safety information.

Desicon Alerts is an official, IT-approved application. The sign-in screen you'll see is the same trusted Microsoft sign-in you already use for your email — please go ahead and sign in as normal.

If you have any questions, or if anything looks unusual, please contact the ICT Help Desk at **[helpdesk email / extension]**.

Thank you for helping us build a better-connected Desicon.
